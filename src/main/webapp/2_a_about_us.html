Digital Survey

Final year project

It is a web project on Digital Survey which is used to conduct a survey.

Here a user have to register himself/herslf and then have to login.

After login user will be redirected to a new dynamic JSP page where his/her profile will be shown.

He will be provided two fetures :-
1-Launch a new Survey.
2-See the list of all survey which has been launched.
